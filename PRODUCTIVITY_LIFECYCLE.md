### LOWER PRIORITY (Nice to Have)

## Success Metrics

**User Activation**: % who complete planning session
**Engagement**: Days active per week
**Momentum**: % who work on project 2+ days in a row
**Completion**: % who finish projects
**Retention**: % who start 2nd project after completing 1st
**NPS**: "How likely to recommend?" after project completion
